Our Mission

Something New Is Rising

There's a sound stirring — raw, honest, unafraid. Sick Minds Group is a new non-denominational Christian band forming in the Denver area, and we're looking for the musicians, vocalists, and creative minds God has been preparing for exactly this moment.

We are not a traditional worship band. We're not confined to one genre, one style, or one congregation. Our music draws from rock, gospel, jazz, hip-hop, folk, and everything in between — because the Spirit moves through all of it. What binds us together isn't a sound. It's a foundation.

We believe the light shines brightest in the darkest places. That faith isn't passive — it's a force. That art, music, and community are acts of worship when they're rooted in truth. We're building something from the ground up: a band, a creative family, and a place where broken people make beautiful noise together.

If you've been sitting on your gift wondering when the right door would open — this might be it.

We're currently seeking musicians of all backgrounds and skill levels who share a heart for Christ and a passion for authentic, genre-bending music. You don't have to have it all figured out. You just have to show up ready to create something real.

We are also actively searching for a rehearsal and recording space in the Denver metro area. If you have a space, know of one, or want to partner with us in building this vision out — we want to hear from you too.

Whether you play piano in a jazz club or shred guitar in a garage — whether you've been in ministry your whole life or you're just finding your faith — there is a seat at this table for you. Come as you are. Bring what you have. Let's build something that matters.
